Simple Process

How AgriLink Works

A seamless process for both farmers and industries to connect and collaborate.

🌱
For Farmers
1
Register Your Farm
Create a profile with your farm details, location, land size and crops you grow.
2
Browse Contracts
Explore contracts posted by verified food companies and exporters across India.
3
Apply & Negotiate
Apply directly to contracts that match your crops. Chat with buyers to negotiate terms.
4
Deliver & Get Paid
Fulfill the contract, track deliveries and receive guaranteed payments.
🏭
For Industries
1
Register Your Company
Create a verified company profile with your procurement requirements and details.
2
Post Crop Requirements
List the crops you need with quantity, quality specs, price and delivery timeline.
3
Review Applications
Farmers apply to your contract. Review profiles, farm size and production capacity.
4
Secure Your Supply Chain
Select the best farmers, finalize agreements and track deliveries in real time.

How AgriLink Works
A simple, transparent process connecting farmers and industries — from registration to delivery and payment.
Step 01
Create Your Farm Profile
Sign up for free and build your farm profile. Add your location, land size, crops you grow, and expected annual production. This helps industries find and match with you.
💡The more detailed your profile, the more contract matches you'll receive.
🌱
🔍
Step 02
Browse the Contract Marketplace
Explore hundreds of active contracts posted by verified food companies, exporters and retailers across India. Filter by crop type, region, price and delivery timeline to find the best fit.
🤖Our AI assistant can suggest contracts perfectly matched to your farm and crops.
Step 03
Apply to Supply
Found a contract that matches? Apply directly with a single click. Share your farm details, production capacity and any questions you have. No agents, no brokers — direct connection.
✉️You can apply to multiple contracts at the same time.
📝
💬
Step 04
Negotiate & Finalize Terms
Once a company shortlists you, connect via our built-in messaging system. Discuss quantity, quality requirements, delivery schedules and pricing. Share documents and certifications securely.
🤝Our AI assistant can help you understand complex contract terms in simple language.
Step 05
Sign Digital Contract
Agree on terms and sign a digital contract directly on the platform. The contract clearly outlines crop quality, quantity, price, delivery location and payment timeline — protecting both parties.
🔒All contracts are stored securely and accessible from your dashboard.
📜
🚚
Step 06
Grow, Harvest & Deliver
Grow your crop as per the agreed quality standards. Track your delivery schedule from your dashboard. Coordinate pickup or delivery with the company using the built-in delivery tracker.
📊The AI assistant gives crop-specific advice to meet quality requirements.
Step 07
Get Paid — Guaranteed
Once delivery is confirmed, payment is released directly to your bank account. Our escrow system ensures you're paid on time, every time. No more delayed payments or disputes.
💰Payments are processed within 48 hours of confirmed delivery.
💰
Step 01
Register Your Company
Create a verified company profile on AgriLink. Add your company details, GST number, industry type and procurement requirements. Verification gives you a trusted badge visible to all farmers.
✔Verified companies get 3x more farmer applications.
🏭
📋
Step 02
Post Your Crop Requirements
Create a detailed contract listing specifying crop type, quality grade, quantity needed, price you're offering, preferred regions and delivery deadline. Your listing goes live immediately to 2,400+ farmers.
💡Clear quality requirements attract better-matched farmers.
Step 03
Review Farmer Applications
Farmers from across India will apply to your contract. Review each application with full farm details — land size, crop history, certifications, ratings and production capacity. All in one place.
🤖Our AI ranks applications by best match to your requirements automatically.
👥
💬
Step 04
Connect & Negotiate
Shortlist promising farmers and connect via our secure messaging system. Discuss terms, verify certifications, share contract documents and finalize the deal — all within the platform.
📁Share PDF contracts, certification documents and photos securely in chat.
Step 05
Select & Sign Agreement
Select the best-fit farmers for your contract and finalize digital agreements. The signed contract is stored on the platform and accessible to both parties at any time.
🔒You can select multiple farmers to meet large quantity requirements.
📜
🗺️
Step 06
Track & Monitor Deliveries
Monitor all your active contracts from your industry dashboard. Track crop growing progress, delivery schedules and quality reports. Get alerts when farmers are ready for pickup or delivery.
📊Analytics dashboard shows your supply coverage and procurement spend.
Step 07
Confirm & Release Payment
Inspect delivered crops, confirm quality and approve the delivery on the platform. Payment is automatically released to the farmer from escrow. Build a reliable, repeat supply chain over time.
🤝Satisfied farmers become long-term reliable suppliers for your business.

Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
Is AgriLink free for farmers?
+
Yes! AgriLink is completely free for farmers to register, browse contracts and apply. We believe every farmer deserves access to fair markets without paying upfront fees. Industry buyers pay a small platform fee only when a deal is successfully completed.
How does AgriLink verify industries and buyers?
+
All industry buyers go through a verification process where we check their GST registration, company documents and business credentials. Verified companies display a blue checkmark on their profile, giving farmers confidence that they're dealing with legitimate buyers.
What happens if a company doesn't pay after delivery?
+
Our payment escrow system holds the buyer's payment before the contract begins. Once the farmer delivers and the delivery is confirmed, the payment is automatically released. This means the money is already secured before the farmer even starts growing.
Can small farmers with limited land also join?
+
Absolutely! AgriLink welcomes farmers of all sizes, including those with under 1 acre of land. Many contracts accept smaller quantities, and we're building a feature for farmers to group together for larger contracts. Every farmer deserves a fair marketplace.
Is the platform available in regional languages?
+
We are actively working on adding Assamese, Bengali, Hindi and other regional languages to make the platform accessible to farmers across all regions of India. The AI assistant can already communicate in multiple languages — just type or speak in your preferred language.
How does the AI assistant help farmers?
+
The AI assistant can explain contract terms in simple language, suggest which crops have high market demand, help you find contracts that match your farm's location and crops, answer questions about the platform, and provide guidance on quality requirements. It's available 24/7 at the bottom right of every page.
